ROADMAP

Project Development

We started this project by building solid foundations: forming a team of dedicated developers and the right business and marketing lead. Together, we sat to organise this fantastic project. After heavy planning and preliminary expenditure, the team are proud to present the Captain Doge token, the BSC’s most rewarding investment for smart, long term investors!

Marketing / Community Building / Pinksale Presale

We will start our journey by launching an awareness campaign, to maximize the visibility of the project in the BSC ecosystem. We are reaching out to all active call-in channels in the space for motivation and we will be providing whitelisted giveaways. The pre-sale will take place on March 5, 2022, at 02PM UTC, with a soft limit of 75 BNB and a hard limit of 150 BNB (maximum contribution 0.8 BNB). If due to the thunder of Thor's hammer we do not reach the 150BNB limit, we will open the presale to the public.

Social Media / Website / Launch

On March 5, 2022, we will launch Captain Doge token, working on BSC. The contract will be listed on PancakeSwap and news and updates regarding the launch will be posted on all of our social media platforms and on our website. Our white paper will be released before launch.

Audit, Listings and post-Launch Marketing

Our first priorities after launch are to secure a Certik or Tech Rate audit, apply for CoinMarketCap and CoinGecko listings and ensure big marketing avenues are exploited. The development revenue secured from transaction taxes will be used as leverage to try and secure partnerships with influencers who we want to invite on board for the long run. Marketing calls are great however relationships are better.

Doge Avengers P2E Game Previews

We are developing the Doge Avengers 2D platform, where players begin their journey by designing their own avatar and then set out to conquer their surroundings, whilst earning Captain Doge tokens for their gameplay. Play individually or form teams, the aim is to complete as many challenges as possible, and to defeat opponents in paid duels. Our game dev will continually introduce new challenges in gameplay. In the DA game, players will be able to challenge other players to a duel, for an agreed upon Captain Doge Token amount. The winner takes all. Players will complete challenges to unlock features, such as individual powers, weapons and costumes/accessories, all based on the Avengers theme. Unlock Thor’s Hammer, the Hulk’s green rage, the Iron Man suit, Spiderman’s various suits and powers etc etc the list goes on.
