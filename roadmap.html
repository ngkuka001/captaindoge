ROADMAP

Project Development

We started this project by building solid foundations: forming a team of dedicated developers and the right business and marketing lead. Together, we sat to organise this fantastic project. After heavy planning and preliminary expenditure, the team are proud to present the Captain Floki token, the BSC’s most rewarding investment for smart, long term investors!

Marketing / Community Building / Pinksale Presale

We will begin our journey by launching an awareness campaign, to maximise project exposure in the BSC ecosystem. We are reaching out to all active call channels in the space to generate momentum and we will offer whitelist giveaways. The presale will take place on the 20th January 2022, at 8PM UTC, with a 150BNB soft cap and 300 BNB hard cap (0.2BNB minimum contribution and 2BNB max contribution). If, by the thunder of Thor’s hammer, we do not reach the 200BNB hard cap, we will open the presale to the public.

Social Media / Website / Launch

On the 21st January 2022, we will launch the Captain Floki token, live on the BSC. The contract will be listed on PancakeSwap and news and updates regarding launch will be posted across all our social media platforms, and on our website. Our white paper will be released pre-Launch.

Audit, Listings and post-Launch Marketing

Our first priorities after launch are to secure a Certik or Tech Rate audit, apply for CoinMarketCap and CoinGecko listings and ensure big marketing avenues are exploited. The development revenue secured from transaction taxes will be used as leverage to try and secure partnerships with influencers who we want to invite on board for the long run. Marketing calls are great however relationships are better.

Floki Avengers P2E Game Previews

We are developing the Floki Avengers 2D platform, where players begin their journey by designing their own avatar and then set out to conquer their surroundings, whilst earning Captain Floki tokens for their gameplay. Play individually or form teams, the aim is to complete as many challenges as possible, and to defeat opponents in paid duels. Our game dev will continually introduce new challenges in gameplay. In the FA game, players will be able to challenge other players to a duel, for an agreed upon Captain Floki Token amount. The winner takes all. Players will complete challenges to unlock features, such as individual powers, weapons and costumes/accessories, all based on the Avengers theme. Unlock Thor’s Hammer, the Hulk’s green rage, the Iron Man suit, Spiderman’s various suits and powers etc etc the list goes on.
